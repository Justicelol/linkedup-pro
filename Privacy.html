LinkedUp – Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the LinkedUp Twitch extension (“Extension”) collects, uses, and shares information. The Extension is operated by the developer known as “LinkedUp.”

1. Information We Receive

The Extension may receive limited Twitch account data, including:

  • Your Twitch user ID and display name
  • Your channel ID
  • Your current Twitch category
  • LFG settings or descriptions you provide through the Extension

We do not receive:

  • Passwords
  • Payment or subscription details
  • Private messages or whispers

2. How We Use This Information

  • Display your creator profile and collaboration status
  • Enable collab request functionality
  • Support category-based matching
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse

3. Data Storage

Most data is stored through Twitch’s Extension Configuration Service (ECS). If additional storage is used (e.g., for collab logs), it is used only to support core Extension functionality.

4. Sharing of Data

  • With other creators (e.g., your display name, category, LFG indicator)
  • With hosting providers or tools necessary to operate the Extension
  • If required by law or legal request

5. Cookies

The Extension does not use third-party advertising cookies. Twitch may use its own cookies according to the Twitch Privacy Notice.

6. Your Choices

You may disable or remove the Extension from your Twitch channel at any time. You may also update your Twitch privacy or security settings directly.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Extension is intended for users who meet Twitch’s minimum age requirements. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy periodically. Continued use after updates signifies acceptance of the revised Policy.
